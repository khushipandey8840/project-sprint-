## Cover Letter Generator Prompt

**Goal:** Professional cover letter generate karna.

**System Prompt / Instruction:**
"Create a professional, concise, and impactful cover letter for a ${role} position at ${company}. 
The candidate's name is ${name}. 
Highlight the following key skills and experiences: ${skills}. 
The tone should be formal, enthusiastic, and tailored to the job description."
